#Race#
	
	Bertle
// VARIANT: Vulper


#Options#
When creating your character:
	Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Wisdom, Intelligence, Dexterity or Charisma
	Choose one: +1 Movement Speed or +2 Dexterity saves
	Choose one: +1 Fortitude Save or +1 Charisma Save or +1 Wisdom Save
	
#Skills#
You have +1 Acrobatics, +1 Perception, +1 Craft
You have -1 Animal Handling, -1 Deception, -1 History

#Stats#
Base Health: 7
Movement: 6 meters
Lifespan: ~75 years
Languages: Common

#Language#
If your Intelligence is at least 2, you can speak 1 additional Common language.

#OTHER#
Due to the variations in Bertle species, you can choose if you want your size to be Small or Medium.

#Abilities#
You start with the following racial ability:

[Herbivore] : Passive
You can not eat meat.
If you do, you take take 1d12 Poison damage and have Disadvantage on all rolls for 1 hour.
You take 50% more damage from Poison (other than when eating meat).

#LevelBonus#
At Level 1, you can pick one of the following talents and know it permanently:

{Fleet Footed} : Passive
You are immune to slows.

{Nimbleness} : Passive
You can move through the spaces of enemy units (in normal conditions).

{Darkvision} : Passive
You can see up to 12 meters away in darkness, without discerning colors.

{Cutting Teeth} : Passive
Your teeth act as natural daggers which can pierce through some materials:
You are able to cut rope, flesh, wood, etc (but not concrete, metal, etc).
Attacking with your teeth takes 1 Action, adds Dexterity modifier and deals 1d8 Piercing damage.

#LevelBonus#
At Level 2 you gain +2 Feat Points.
And don't forget to choose a Talent from your class!

#LevelBonus#

Your maximum Health also increses by 1 + Your Fortitude, in addition to the maximum Health you usually gain when leveling!
Also, pick one of the following Talents and know it permanently:

Also, you get +2 Feat Points and don't forget to choose a Talent from your class!

	At Level 3:

{Protruded Claws} : Passive
You gain +3 on Athletics rolls for Climbing.
You become Trained in Unarmed (if you aren't already) and can do Unarmed attacks with any Stat instead of Dexterity/Fortitude.


{Skipper} : Passive
You can freely jump up to 2 meters in length when moving (still uses 2 meters of your movement speed).
Doing this allows you to freely jump over small obstacles, gaps, etc without having to roll.
# If playing with grid, you can jump over a square and land in the next one. #





{Thick Fur} : Passive
You have 50% resistance to Cold damage.

{Sidekick} : 1 Action
Make a melee attack against a creature with a total of +4 to the roll.
If you hit, you deal 1d6 damage, you can Shift 1 meter in any possible direction, and the target rolls a Fortitude save.
If it fails, it is stunned.
You can do this once per Long Rest.

{High Roller} : 0 Actions
Choose one:
1. Gain Advantage on an Acrobatics roll
2. Gain +3 meters movement this turn
You can do this once per Long Rest.



{Gatherer Instincts} : Passive
You are no longer Vulnerable to Poison.
You have +5 on identifying plants, fruits, mushrooms, vegetables, etc.
