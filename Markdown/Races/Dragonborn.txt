#Race#

	Dragonborn
// VARIANT: Loxen

#Options#
When creating your character:
	Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Fortitude, Wisdom, Intelligence or Charisma
	Choose one: +3 Health or 1 known spell from the Amateur Spell List
	Choose one: +1 Fortitude Save or +1 Charisma Save or +1 Wisdom Save
	
#Skills#
You have +1 Arcana, +1 Athletics, +1 Intimidation
You have -1 Stealth, -1 Luck, -1 Nature

#Stats#
Base Health: 6
Movement: 6 meters
Lifespan: ~80 years
Languages: Common
Size: Large

#Language#
If your Intelligence is at least 1, you can speak 1 additional Common language.

#Abilities#
You start with the following racial abilities:

[Draconic Ancestry] : Special
When creating your character, choose an element: Fire, Frost (Cold), Lightning (Thunder), Poison or Acid.
That becomes your Dragonborn Element.

[Dragonborn Resistance] : Passive
You have 50% resistance to damage of your Dragonborn Element type.

[Dragon's Breath] : 1 Action
Breathe your Dragonborn Element in a 3x1 meter line.
All creatures caught in your breath must succeed a DC10 Dexterity save or take 2d6 damage of your Dragonborn Element type.



#LevelBonus#
At Level 1, you can pick one of the following talents and know it permanently:

{Breath: Cone} : Special
Your Dragon's Breath now breathes in a 3 meter, 45* cone instead of a line.

{Breath: Line} : Special
Your Dragon's Breath now breathes in a 4x2 meter line.

{Breath: Blast} : Special
Your Dragon's Breath now breathes on all creatures around you instead of a line.

#LevelBonus#
At Level 2 you gain +2 Feat Points.
And don't forget to choose a Talent from your class!

#LevelBonus#
At Level 3, your maximum number of Charges increases by +1.
Your maximum Health also increses by 1 + Your Fortitude, in addition to the maximum Health you usually gain when leveling!
Also, pick one of the following Talents and know it permanently:

#LevelBonus#
At Level 4 you gain the Action Surge ability. Use it wisely!

[Action Surge] : 0 Actions
Gain an extra 1 Action this turn!
You can do this once per Long Rest.

Also, you get +2 Feat Points and don't forget to choose a Talent from your class!

#LevelBonus#
At Level 5, your maximum number of Charges increases by +1.
Also, pick one of the following Talents and know it permanently:
If you are a Spellcaster, take a new look at your spells. You probably just unlocked some Level 5 spells!

#LevelBonus#
At Level 6, choose any of your 5 base Stats and increase it by +1 (up to a maximum of +4).
Also, you get +2 Feat Points and don't forget to choose a Talent from your class!

#LevelBonus#
At Level 7, your maximum number of Charges increases by +1.
Your maximum Health increses by 1 + Your Fortitude, in addition to the maximum Health you usually gain when leveling!
Also, pick one of the following Talents and know it permanently:

#LevelBonus#
At Level 8, you gain +2 Feat Points and don't forget to choose a Talent from your class.

#LevelBonus#
At Level 9, your maximum number of Charges increases by +1.
Also, pick one of the following Talents and know it permanently:
If you are a Spellcaster, take a new look at your spells. You probably just unlocked some Level 5 spells!

#LevelBonus#
At Level 10, you have reached the peak of your skill.
Congratulations!
But don't forget to choose a Talent from your class.
It will be a powerful one!


#PageEnd#

	At Level 3:
{Breath Master} : Special
Dragon's Breath does not affect allies and its save DC increases by 2.

{Scaleskin} : Passive
When Unarmored, you can apply Fortitude instead of Dexterity to your Armor bonus.
You also have +1 Armor when Unarmored.

{Double Breath} : Passive
You can use Dragon's Breath twice per Long Rest.

{Tail Swing} : 1 Action
Perform an attack with your tail on a creature (2 meters range, +4 to hit).
If you hit, deal 1d8 + 1 Smash damage and that creature rolls a Fortitude save.
If it fails, it is either pushed up to 1 meter away from you and Stunned.

{Flaming Jaws} : 0.5 Action
Leap at a target up to 4 meters away from you ignoring attacks of oportunity.
You can then perform an attack with your jaws on the target (melee, +4 to hit).
If you hit, deal 3 damage (of your Dragonborn Element) and heal for 3 health.
